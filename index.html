Projects & Practicals

Research Project: Solar Golf Car with Battery-Capacitor Hybrid Technology

Supervisor: Prof. Rupert Gouws

Designed and developed a solar-powered golf cart prototype using a LiFePO4 battery and supercapacitor hybrid system, regulated via MPPT charge control. Implemented a fuzzy logic-based energy management system to dynamically switch power sources based on torque demand, monitored via sensors.

Goal: To achieve extended runtimes and improved power efficiency during high-current demanding conditions such as acceleration and hill climbing.
